MEETINGS

Throughout my internship, I had the privilege of meeting with many amazing people. From engineering, to marketing, to product, to people, I gained insight into many different parts of the company. I lunched and learned with the CEO’s of Zume Pizza as well as Zume Inc., VP’s of Research, People, Product, Engineering, and many more. Each person I met was very encouraging with stories to tell and advice to give.

Speaking of meetings, I attended plenty of those. I spent a few days a week shadowing the Director of Product Management where I learned about Agile Development. While in those meetings, I was able to be a part of the engineering teams and learn a lot about what they do on the back end of the business. I grew to love the "behind the scenes" part of Zume, the masterminds behind the robots. All the coding and hours of bug fixing that went into the final product. But I’m getting ahead of myself, more on that later.

While their jobs all interested me, my favorite part about each meeting was how they got to Zume. Different people all from different backgrounds working together as ONE TEAM.
